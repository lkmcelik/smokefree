PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: March 23, 2023

1. INTRODUCTION

We respect your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app. If you don't agree with these terms, please don't use the app.

2. THE APP WORKS OFFLINE ONLY

SmokeFree works entirely on your device. We don't collect or store your data on any servers.

Your Data Stays On Your Device

All your information stays on your phone, including:

  • Your name
  • Your smoking habits
  • Your health information

None of this data is sent to us or anyone else.

No Internet Connection Needed

The app never:

  • Connects to servers
  • Sends data over the internet
  • Tracks how you use it
  • Tracks your location

Notifications

The app may send you notifications about your progress. These are created on your device only.

3. APP STORE PURCHASES

When you buy something through the App Store:

  • Apple handles the payment
  • We don't see your payment details
  • We only know a purchase was made
  • Your purchase info stays on your device

4. KEEPING YOUR DATA SAFE

Since your data stays on your device, we recommend:

  • Using a passcode or Face ID/Touch ID
  • Updating your device regularly
  • Being careful about who uses your device

5. CHILDREN

This app is not for children under 17.

6. DATA BACKUP & DELETION

Your app data may be included in your device backups. To delete all app data, just uninstall the app.

7. POLICY UPDATES

We may update this policy. Check it when you update the app.
